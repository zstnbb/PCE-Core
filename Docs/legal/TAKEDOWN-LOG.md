# PCE Takedown Log

> **Status**: active — append-only public record of resolved legal events.
>
> **Authority**: [`CEASE-AND-DESIST-RESPONSE.md`](CEASE-AND-DESIST-RESPONSE.md) §6.
>
> **Purpose**: Provide a sanitized, auditable record of legal notices received and actions taken. This log serves as evidence of good-faith compliance and rebuts any future claim of "willful" conduct.
>
> **Privacy**: All entries are redacted of personal information, internal legal strategy, and privileged communications. Only the public-facing facts (date, sender category, scope, outcome) are recorded.

---

## Format

Each entry follows this template:

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — [Sender Category] — [Scope]

- **Type**: Cease-and-Desist / DMCA §512 / Webstore Takedown / Informal / Other
- **Sender category**: AI service provider / IDE vendor / Browser platform / Rightsholder / Other
- **Scope**: Which PCE component was targeted
- **Severity**: P0 / P1 / P2 / P3 (per CEASE-AND-DESIST-RESPONSE.md §1.3)
- **Action taken**: [what we did]
- **Outcome**: Resolved / Pending / Counter-notice filed / Withdrawn by sender
- **Days to resolution**: N
```
